Professional Services

Our Services

A single platform for audit, taxation, compliance, advisory, and financial consulting — tailored to the realities of your business.

Audit & Assurance

Independent audit and assurance services designed to enhance financial transparency, strengthen reporting systems, and ensure regulatory compliance. Our services include Statutory Audits, Tax Audits, Internal Audits, Management Audits, Concurrent Audits, Due Diligence Reviews, and Assurance Engagements in accordance with applicable accounting and regulatory standards. We assist businesses in strengthening internal financial controls, improving reporting accuracy, and building stakeholder confidence through reliable and transparent financial practices.

Direct & Indirect Taxation

Comprehensive support in Income Tax and GST matters, including tax planning, compliance, return filings, assessments, advisory, and litigation support. Our team assists individuals, firms, companies, and other entities in managing tax obligations efficiently while ensuring compliance with evolving tax laws and regulations. Services also include GST registration, GST audits, notices and scrutiny handling, TDS/TCS compliances, international taxation support, and representation before tax authorities.

Business Advisory & Consulting

Strategic consulting services aimed at supporting business growth, operational efficiency, and long-term sustainability. We provide practical and result-oriented advisory solutions in areas such as business structuring, financial planning, process optimization, budgeting, profitability analysis, and strategic decision-making. Our objective is to help organizations improve performance, manage risks effectively, and achieve sustainable business growth.

Project Finance & Banking Support

End-to-end assistance in arranging project finance and banking support for startups, SMEs, and established enterprises. Our services include preparation of CMA data, financial projections, DPRs (Detailed Project Reports), loan documentation, working capital assessment, bank liaisoning, and assistance in securing term loans, cash credit facilities, and other financial arrangements. We help businesses present strong financial proposals and improve funding accessibility.

Forensic & Investigation Services

Specialized forensic audit and investigation services focused on identifying financial irregularities, fraud detection, risk assessment, and strengthening internal controls. We assist organizations in conducting forensic reviews, transaction analysis, fund tracing, dispute investigations, and compliance assessments. Our approach helps businesses safeguard assets, enhance accountability, and minimize financial and operational risks.

Internal Financial Systems

Advisory and implementation support for robust internal financial systems and operational controls. We assist businesses in designing and improving accounting processes, internal control mechanisms, MIS reporting systems, SOPs, budgeting frameworks, and financial workflows. Our services are aimed at enhancing operational efficiency, improving financial monitoring, and enabling informed decision-making through accurate financial information.

Financial Restructuring & Rehabilitation

Customized financial restructuring and rehabilitation solutions for businesses facing operational or financial challenges. We assist in debt restructuring, cash flow management, financial reorganization, cost optimization, revival planning, and strategic restructuring initiatives. Our objective is to help businesses regain financial stability, improve operational performance, and create a sustainable path for future growth.

Company Formation & Regulatory Registration

Complete assistance in the incorporation and registration of Companies, LLPs, Partnership Firms, Trusts, NGOs, and other business entities. Our services cover name approval, drafting of incorporation documents, regulatory registrations, PAN/TAN applications, startup registrations, and post-incorporation compliances. We ensure a smooth and legally compliant setup process tailored to the client’s business objectives and operational requirements.

Corporate Compliance & Secretarial Support

Comprehensive support in corporate law compliances and secretarial matters under applicable regulatory frameworks. Our services include ROC filings, annual compliances, board and shareholder documentation, maintenance of statutory records, drafting of resolutions, event-based filings, and advisory on company law matters. We help businesses maintain regulatory compliance while ensuring proper corporate governance practices.

Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)

Advisory services focused on establishing effective governance structures, identifying organizational risks, and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. We assist businesses in developing compliance frameworks, risk assessment mechanisms, policy documentation, internal control reviews, and governance practices aligned with industry standards. Our GRC solutions help organizations strengthen accountability, improve risk management, and build a culture of compliance and transparency.
